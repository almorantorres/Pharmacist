Description of the career


Image from Further Along the Road.

A pharmacist, aka chemists or druggists, that hand out prescription medications to patients and give expert advice on how to use the prescriptions. They also assess the patient's conditions, monitor disease states, supervise pharmacy technicians, and provide pharmaceutical information. The most comon types of pharmacists are the retail pharmacists and the hospital pharmacists.
