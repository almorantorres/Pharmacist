Daily tasks preformed by pharmacists


Image from Healthcare-salaries and Shutterstock

Pharmacists are normally the first first ones that are contacted by patients with questions regarding someones health. This makes pharmacist have a significant role in managing the amount of medicine that the patient takes, and transferring them to a doctors.

The many roles that doctors have include the following:

  • Regulation of medicine being taken by patient, and observation of amount taken.
  • Analysis of patients who were diagnosed/not diagnosed and answering any medical questions that they may have.
  • Providing pharmaceutical information
  • Providing patients with information and advice, including advice that has to do with common ailments and diseases.
  • Supervising over pharmacy technicians and over other staff members.
  • Information for patients and other health care providers on the proper use of medications (proper dosage, as to avoid over-medication).
  • Providing over-the-counter drugs, and non-prescription drugs.
  • Careful monetoring over the disease state of a patient.

How computer science is used in Pharmacy:

Computer significantly shortens the time, expenditure, and manpower required for any kind of work. Various softwares makes it trouble-free to handle huge amounts of data. Computers have an essential role in pharmacy field, without computers pharmacy research will take a much longer time and expensive.

Salary:

The medium yearly wage for pharmacists was $116,670 in May 2012. The medium wage is the wage at which half the workers in an occupation earned more than that amount and half earned less. The lowest 10% earned less than $89,280 a year, and the top 10% percent earned more than $145,910 a year. The medians wage was abput $56.09 an hour.
